# MarketMasters

## What It Is

An education and trust platform that reaches agents outside Benson Crew. Through trainings, events, seminars, and community content, it puts Benson Crew leadership on stage and makes the team more visible without leading with a recruiting pitch.

## What It Does

- Hosts trainings and events for agents not yet on the team
- Builds trust through education, not sales pitches
- Extends reach beyond Benson Crew's immediate boundary
- Gives leaders, downline partners, and referrals a value-first way to start the conversation
- Hands the direct recruiting conversation to Benson Crew or Real when the timing is right

## What It Is NOT

- Not a direct-response recruiting funnel
- Not a place for internal wins or performance bragging.
- Not Benson Crew content under a different name.

## The Decision Test

"Is this building trust, or delivering execution?"

Trust = MarketMasters. Execution = Benson Crew. If it blurs, kill it.

If the main point is to recruit or close directly, it belongs in Benson Crew or a direct recruiting conversation, not here.

## How It Connects to the Broader Strategy

MarketMasters builds trust that can lead to two outcomes:

1. Some agents join Benson Crew directly.
2. Many others join Real Broker through the Benson Crew downline.

Both outcomes serve the Agent Engine. Its job is to warm the conversation and hand it off cleanly, not carry the close itself.

## Tone

Educational. Trust-first. Opt-in. Never high-pressure.
