# Quarterly Priorities

## Current Quarter: Q1 2026 (Feb - Apr)

Making the engine operate predictably. Not redefining strategy.

Benson Crew quarters run one month off standard: Q1 = Feb-Apr, Q2 = May-Jul, Q3 = Aug-Oct, Q4 = Nov-Jan. Planning happens in the month before.

---

## Priority 1: Lock Leadership Ownership and Execution Boundaries

**Owner:** Steve

Winning looks like:

- One clear owner per vital function
- 3-5 KPIs per function tied to the Agent Engine
- Clear handoffs: Marketing to Recruiting to Ops to Sales
- Leaders bring KPI movement and plans, not task updates
- Execution problems surface where they occur, not at Steve's desk

Failing indicator: Issues escalate to Steve instead of being resolved at department level. If ownership is unclear for 2+ weeks, structural change is forced.

---

## Priority 2: Build a Predictable Recruiting Engine

**Owner:** Steve + Scott | **Contributors:** Tanner, Carson

Winning looks like:

- Recruiting pace visible weekly against target
- Small-team acquisition and individual recruiting produce consistent conversations
- MarketMasters creates qualified recruiting attention
- Benson Crew converts through proof, standards, and execution
- Every "yes" moves immediately into onboarding
- Missed pace triggers structural response, not discussion

Failing indicator: Recruiting pace behind target for 2 consecutive weeks. Behind 1 month triggers structural change, not more effort.

---

## Priority 3: Make Benson Crew a Compounding Growth Brand

**Owner:** Tanner | **Contributors:** Steve, Georgia, Carson

Winning looks like:

- Founder thought leadership is visible and consistent
- Benson Crew proof-of-execution content circulates regularly
- MarketMasters adds trust-led recruiting attention without blurring the brand
- Visible proof of execution published consistently
- Agents reuse and benefit from brand assets
- Recruiting conversations naturally reference brand proof
- Retention signals stable or improving
- Brand activity reduces individual effort, not just creates more activity

Failing indicator: Brand assets not being reused by agents within 2 weeks of creation.
