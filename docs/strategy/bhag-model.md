# BHAG Model

## Team Goal: $2B

$2B is Benson Crew team sales volume by 2031. The source-backed panel below shows the current milestone path from `SRC-FREEDOM-BHAG-001`.

The model depends on scaling agent capacity while protecting per-agent productivity. Required agent counts, production targets, and timelines are calculated in the Freedom KPI Sheet (`SRC-FREEDOM-BHAG-001`) and recalculate when assumptions change.

## Community Goal: 10,000 Agents

The 10,000-agent goal is the combined Benson Crew leadership downline at Real Broker, not 10,000 Benson Crew team members.

The source-backed panel below shows the current combined downline snapshot from `SRC-FREEDOM-COMMUNITY-001`.

Real Broker is the brokerage/platform. Benson Crew operates inside that brokerage as a team. Agents may work directly with Real Broker or inside teams led by team leaders within the brokerage. The downline goal depends on Benson Crew proving a model that makes the broader Real Broker ecosystem more attractive.

Downline growth happens while the team is being built, but it becomes a dedicated scaling focus only after the Benson Crew platform is strong enough to support it intentionally.

In the Freedom Sheet community tracker, team/member data lives in columns `A:E` (`SRC-FREEDOM-TEAM-001`). Real Broker community and downline growth data lives in `G:O` (`SRC-FREEDOM-COMMUNITY-001`). Community revenue by leader plus `Bcrew In Before HST` lives in `P:U` (`SRC-FREEDOM-COMMUNITY-REV-001`).

## How the Math Works

The Freedom KPI Sheet (`SRC-FREEDOM-ENGINE-001`) is the live calculator:

- Target GCI per active agent (currently $13K/month)
- Target split (currently 50%)
- Required monthly attraction rate calculated from the gap between current agents and target
- If any assumption changes, the entire engine recalculates

"Active agents" means agents who produce, not agents on the roster. The baseline was reset September 2025 when leadership got honest about who was real.
