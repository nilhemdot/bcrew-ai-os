## ATTRACT Avatars (Agent Recruiting)

### ATTRACT-1: The Stuck Producer
**Who:** Agent doing 3-15 deals/year, 3-8 years in, working hard but plateauing
**Pain:** "I'm doing everything alone." Inconsistent income, no systems, watching peers pass them
**Emotions:** Frustration, self-doubt, exhaustion
**Trigger words:** stuck, plateau, grinding, spinning wheels, no leads, no support, no systems
**Content that works:** Before/after case studies with real numbers, day-in-the-life showing systems, income breakdowns after fees

### ATTRACT-2: The New Agent
**Who:** 0-2 years licensed, overwhelmed, considering quitting
**Pain:** "Nobody taught me how to actually do this." Zero leads, inadequate training
**Emotions:** Overwhelm, impostor syndrome, desperation
**Trigger words:** nobody taught me, don't know where to start, considering quitting, first year
**Content that works:** Day-in-the-life, mentorship proof, "what I wish I knew" content

### ATTRACT-3: The Experienced Agent (Ceiling Hitter)
**Who:** 50+ deals, wants leverage, tired of doing everything solo
**Pain:** "I need a team behind me so I can scale." Capacity ceiling
**Emotions:** Ambition, frustration at ceiling, desire for leverage
**Trigger words:** scale, leverage, capacity, doing everything myself, team behind me
**Content that works:** Infrastructure demos, production comparison data, team resources

### ATTRACT-4: The Team Leader
**Who:** Running a mini-team, needs infrastructure they can't build
**Pain:** "I can't build the systems my team needs." Tech and ops gap
**Emotions:** Responsibility burden, tech frustration, leadership isolation
**Trigger words:** my team needs, can't build, infrastructure, systems for agents
**Content that works:** Platform capability showcases, technology/AI demos

### ATTRACT-5: The Brokerage Refugee
**Who:** Just left or actively leaving a bad brokerage, shopping around
**Pain:** "I know what I DON'T want. Show me different." Skeptical of promises
**Emotions:** Skepticism, wariness, hope mixed with distrust
**Trigger words:** empty promises, show me different, what do you actually offer, prove it
**Content that works:** Transparent comparisons, testimonials from switchers, no-BS proof

## RETAIN Avatars (Client Content)

### RETAIN-1: The Move-Up Family
**Who:** Family outgrowing starter home, 1-3 kids, equity built, 32-44
**Pain:** "Can we afford the upgrade?" Terrified of bridge financing, being house-poor
**Emotions:** Fear, paralysis, hope
**Trigger words:** outgrown, afford the upgrade, house-poor, sell first or buy first, bridge financing

### RETAIN-2: The Divorce Seller
**Who:** Going through separation, forced sale, emotional + legal complexity
**Pain:** Privacy, fairness, speed. Wants it handled with discretion
**Emotions:** Grief, anger, urgency, vulnerability
**Trigger words:** privacy, fair split, how fast, legal, separation, equalization

### RETAIN-3: The Motivated Seller
**Who:** Relocation, downsizing, estate, life transition with time pressure
**Pain:** Speed and certainty. Cannot wait for the perfect buyer
**Emotions:** Urgency, stress, decision fatigue
**Trigger words:** need to sell fast, relocating, estate sale, timeline, conditional vs firm

### RETAIN-4: The First-Time Buyer
**Who:** Never bought before, overwhelmed, affordability anxiety, 25-38
**Pain:** "I don't know where to start." Information overload
**Emotions:** Overwhelm, excitement mixed with fear, affordability panic
**Trigger words:** first time, where to start, how much do I need, FHSA, mortgage pre-approval

### RETAIN-5: The Empty Nester Downsizer
**Who:** Kids gone, house too big, wants lifestyle change, 55-70
**Pain:** Emotional attachment vs practical reality. Hard to let go
**Emotions:** Nostalgia, liberation, fear of change
**Trigger words:** downsize, too much house, kids are gone, condo vs townhouse, maintenance

### RETAIN-6: The Real Estate Investor
**Who:** ROI-focused, multi-property, tax strategy, 32-55
**Pain:** "Show me the numbers, not the pitch." Market timing, rental yield, financing
**Emotions:** Analytical, risk-aware, opportunity-seeking
**Trigger words:** ROI, cap rate, rental yield, investment property, tax implications, cash flow

### RETAIN-7: The Relocating Professional
**Who:** Moving to Ontario for work, zero local knowledge, 28-45
**Pain:** Everything is unknown. Need a trusted local guide in 8 weeks
**Emotions:** Anxiety, disorientation, time pressure
**Trigger words:** moving to Ontario, don't know the area, best neighborhoods, commute, schools

### RETAIN-8: Mortgage Renewal Shock
**Who:** 1.15M Canadians renewing in 2026, payment going up 40-60%, pandemic buyers
**Pain:** "Can I still afford my home?" Panic about rate jump
**Emotions:** Panic, denial, desperation
**Trigger words:** renewal shock, payment going up, can't afford, refinance, should I sell, IRD penalty

### RETAIN-9: Inherited Property
**Who:** Unexpected estate, no idea what to do, tax and sibling complexity, 40-65
**Pain:** Confusion + grief + legal complexity. Siblings as co-beneficiaries
**Emotions:** Grief, confusion, overwhelm, guilt about selling
**Trigger words:** inherited, estate, capital gains, what do I do with this property, probate

### RETAIN-10: New Build vs Resale Buyer
**Who:** Comparing pre-construction vs resale, HST waiver confusion, 27-42
**Pain:** "Which is actually the better deal?" Analysis paralysis
**Emotions:** Analysis paralysis, FOMO, confusion
**Trigger words:** new build vs resale, HST waiver, pre-construction, which is better, Tarion warranty
