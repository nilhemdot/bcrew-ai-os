# Avatar Profiles

Research-backed client and agent personas that drive all content in the marketing pipeline.

## Files

| File | Avatars | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| retain-avatars.md | 10 RETAIN (client) avatars | Buyers & sellers in Southern Ontario |
| attract-avatars.md | 5 ATTRACT (agent) avatars | Licensed agents considering BCrew |

## Last Researched: 2026-04-02

Each avatar includes:
1. Demographics & psychographics
2. Pain points & triggers
3. Actual search queries (Google, Reddit, ChatGPT)
4. Language they use (real forum quotes)
5. Content that moves them (format + angle + proof)
6. Platform behavior (where they scroll)
7. Objections (what stops them)
8. Buying signals (what shows intent)

## RETAIN Avatars (10)

1. Move-Up Family -- "Can we afford the upgrade?"
2. Divorce Seller -- Privacy, fairness, speed
3. Motivated Seller -- Speed and certainty under pressure
4. First-Time Buyer -- "I don't know where to start"
5. Empty Nester Downsizer -- Emotional attachment vs practical reality
6. Real Estate Investor -- "Show me the numbers"
7. Relocating Professional -- Everything is unknown
8. Mortgage Renewal Shock -- Panic, 1.15M renewals in 2026
9. Inherited Property -- Confusion + grief + legal complexity
10. New Build vs Resale -- "Which is actually better for me?"

## ATTRACT Avatars (5)

1. Stuck Producer (3-15 deals) -- "I'm doing everything alone and plateauing"
2. New Agent (0-2 years) -- "Nobody taught me how to actually do this"
3. Experienced Agent (50+ deals) -- "I need a team behind me so I can scale"
4. Team Leader -- "I can't build the systems my team needs"
5. Brokerage Refugee -- "I know what I DON'T want. Show me different."

## How These Are Used

- **Content Writer** reads relevant avatar before generating content
- **Platform Scopers** reference avatar + platform behavior for content direction
- **Avatar Researcher agents** (planned) will refresh these monthly
- **Marketing Director** uses cross-avatar insights for campaign briefs

## Freshness

These profiles should be refreshed monthly by Avatar Researcher agents.
Major market events (rate changes, policy shifts) should trigger ad-hoc updates.
