# Benson Crew Business Strategy

## North Star

- Build Benson Crew into a **$2B real estate team** by 2031
- Use that proof to grow a **10,000-agent Real Broker downline** by 2034
- Benson Crew is the team. Real Broker is the brokerage and compliance platform. Different things.
- The team proves the model works. The downline scales because the proof is visible.

## The Engine

One scoreboard. Three pillars. Everything traces back here.

- **ATTRACT** — Add capacity-producing agents at the required pace
- **GROW** — Protect agent productivity ($13K/month target GCI)
- **RETAIN** — Keep strong agents, intervene early, reduce avoidable loss

If a metric doesn't support Attract, Grow, or Retain, it's not a leadership metric.

## Two Brands

- **Benson Crew** — The destination. Execution, standards, coaching, accountability, outcomes.
- **MarketMasters** — The trust layer. Education, credibility, invitation. Non-salesy, opt-in.
- One direction only: MarketMasters builds trust, Benson Crew delivers. Never blur them.

## Leadership Focus

- Recruiting leverage
- Capital and finance
- Brand leverage
- Decisions

Not execution. If leadership is executing by default, the system is failing.

## Governance

- Weekly execution meeting (60 min)
- Monthly strategic review (90 min, first Friday)
- Department 1:1s (weekly or bi-weekly)
- Quarterly planning and reset

Escalation flows through this cadence, not through habitual upward rescue.

## Rules

1. This document is the source of truth for business direction
2. Live KPI values belong in source systems, not here
3. Decisions that change strategy get logged as explicit records
4. Strategy docs update through tracked proposals and approvals, not silent edits
5. Supporting docs expand this document but never contradict it
6. Benson Crew = team. Real Broker = brokerage/platform. Always preserve that distinction.

## What the System Does

- Reads reality from source systems
- Compares reality to this strategy
- Detects drift, blockers, and opportunities
- Routes intelligence to the right people
- Logs strategic decisions and changes
- Keeps the strategy layer current and trustworthy

The system does not set strategy. It operationalizes leadership strategy.
